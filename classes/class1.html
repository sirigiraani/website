How to Get Started

Welcome to our offline Class program! Here's how students can begin their learning journey:

  1. Registration: Visit our learning center to complete the registration form for creating a slot for the student.
  2. Orientation Session: Attend the mandatory orientation to meet teachers, understand the curriculum, and get your class schedule.
  3. Materials Collection: Collect textbooks, workbooks, and other study materials should be carried by the students during the class schedule.
  4. Class Attendance: Join our engaging offline classes held at our center, designed to foster interactive learning.
  5. Regular Assessments: Participate in periodic assessments to track progress and receive personalized feedback.

Your Future with Us

Our Class program lays a strong foundation for future academic success. Here's what lies ahead:

  • Progressive Curriculum: As you complete Class, you'll advance in these classes, building on your foundational skills in all subjects like Math, English, and Science...etc.
  • Skill Development: Develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills through hands-on activities and group projects.
  • Personalized Support: Our dedicated teachers provide ongoing guidance to ensure you're ready for future challenges.
  • Extracurricular Opportunities: Participate in science fairs, literary clubs, and math Olympiads to explore your interests.
  • Pathway to Higher Education: Our program prepares you for a seamless transition to higher grades, equipping you with the confidence and knowledge to excel.
